Key terms

Bill of sale

A document that records the sale and basic terms of a vehicle purchase.

In the shop: Completing a bill of sale when a used vehicle changes owners.

Lien

A legal claim against a vehicle because of debt; it must be cleared before sale.

In the shop: Checking if a vehicle has a lien before buying.

VIN

Vehicle Identification Number used to identify a specific vehicle.

In the shop: Matching the VIN on the dash to paperwork.

Vehicle history report

A report showing recorded incidents, registrations, and sometimes service history.

In the shop: Reviewing a history report to avoid accident write-offs.

Odometer disclosure

A statement of the vehicle’s mileage at time of sale.

In the shop: Recording mileage accurately on sale documents.

Safety inspection

A check to confirm a vehicle meets minimum safety standards.

In the shop: Using an inspection list to evaluate brakes/tires/steering.

Road test

A controlled drive to assess vehicle condition and symptoms.

In the shop: Testing for vibration, pull, noises, and shift quality.

Pre-purchase inspection

A detailed inspection completed before purchasing a used vehicle.

In the shop: Checking leaks, wear, codes, and maintenance needs.

Recall

A manufacturer repair program to correct a safety/quality issue.

In the shop: Searching for open recalls on a used vehicle.

As-is sale

A sale where the buyer accepts current condition with limited/no warranty.

In the shop: Explaining “as-is” risks to a buyer.

Warranty

A promise that certain repairs will be covered for a time/mileage.

In the shop: Understanding what an aftermarket warranty includes.

Negotiation

The process of agreeing on a price and terms.

In the shop: Using inspection findings to justify a lower offer.

Market value

Typical selling price for similar vehicles in your area.

In the shop: Comparing listings to estimate a fair price.

Total cost of ownership

The full cost including fuel, repairs, insurance, and depreciation.

In the shop: Choosing a vehicle based on expected maintenance costs.

Depreciation

Loss of vehicle value over time.

In the shop: Explaining why newer vehicles drop value faster.
