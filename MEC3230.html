Key terms

Advanced refinishing

High-quality finish work including complex blends and defect correction.

In the shop: Refinishing multiple panels to match colour and gloss.

Tri-coat/three-stage

Paint with base + midcoat + clear; needs careful matching.

In the shop: Matching pearl white tri-coat on a repair.

Pearl/metallic orientation

How flakes/pearls lay affects colour and flop.

In the shop: Adjusting gun distance and overlap to match flop.

Tinting

Adjusting colour formula to match a vehicle.

In the shop: Adding tint based on spray-out comparisons.

Spray-out and variant selection

Using test panels and formula variants for best match.

In the shop: Choosing a variant that matches in sunlight.

Blending strategy

Planning where and how to blend to hide differences.

In the shop: Blending into roof rail or adjacent panel.

Edge mapping

Visible outline of repair due to poor blending or prep.

In the shop: Avoiding hard edges and improper sanding transitions.

Denib and polish process

Removing defects in clear and restoring gloss.

In the shop: Nib sand with fine grit, then compound and polish.

Texture matching

Matching OEM orange peel or texture.

In the shop: Adjusting clear application to match finish.

Spot repair

Small localized repair with controlled blend.

In the shop: Repairing a small chip without full panel repaint.

Contamination control

Preventing dust, silicone, and moisture issues.

In the shop: Cleaning booth, tack cloth, and proper gun cleaning.

Waterborne basecoat

Basecoat system that uses water as main solvent.

In the shop: Using proper airflow and flash time for waterborne.

Booth management

Using correct temperature, airflow, and bake cycles.

In the shop: Setting bake cycle for clearcoat cure.

Final inspection

Checking colour match, gloss, texture, and defects under proper lighting.

In the shop: Inspecting under sunlight and colour-correct lights.

Documentation

Recording products, batch, and procedures for repeatability.

In the shop: Noting paint code, variant, and gun settings.
